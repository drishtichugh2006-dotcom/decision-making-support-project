# Empowering Young Women with Structured Decision-Making Support

## 📌 Overview

This is a group project aimed at empowering young women by providing a structured approach to decision-making. The project focuses on simplifying complex choices through clear frameworks that improve confidence and clarity.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Many young individuals, especially women, face challenges in decision-making due to lack of structured guidance, clarity, and confidence.

---

## 💡 Objective

To design a simple and effective decision-making framework that:

* Breaks down complex decisions into manageable steps
* Encourages logical and confident thinking
* Supports better personal and professional choices

---

## ⚙️ Methodology

Our team followed a structured approach:

1. Identified common decision-making challenges
2. Researched existing models and techniques
3. Designed a simplified framework
4. Applied the framework to real-life scenarios

---

## 🚀 Features

* Step-by-step decision-making model
* Easy-to-understand structure
* Practical and user-focused approach
* Focus on empowerment and confidence

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Presentation Tools (PowerPoint)
* Research-based approach

---

## 📊 Project Outcome

The project provides a clear and structured way to approach decisions, helping users reduce confusion and improve confidence in their choices.
